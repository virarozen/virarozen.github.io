What is a how-to? When you ask someone what a how-to is, they’re likely to give you an answer that includes the words “it’s just a guide” or “it’s a step by step process.” But what exactly is a how-to? How do they differ fromutorials? And why are they so popular? A how-to is simply a guide that provides step-by-step instructions on how to complete a task. They can be found in magazines, newspapers, and even online. Tutorials, on the other hand, are more comprehensive and provide more information than a how-to. Both types of guides can be helpful, but how-tos tend to be shorter and easier to follow.
