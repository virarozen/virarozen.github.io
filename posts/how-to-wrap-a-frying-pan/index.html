A how-to is typically meant to be used as a step-by-step guide that tells readers what they need to do in order to achieve a certain goal. A tutorial, however, is designed to teach readers the ins and outs of a certain topic or subject, so they can learn on their own. A how-to might tell you how to make French toast, while a tutorial would teach you all the different ways you can make french toast - from using an oven mitts to flipping it over multiple times.
